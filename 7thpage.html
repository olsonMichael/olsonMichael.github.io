Internet Dilemmas-IPv6

This is my Internet Dilemmas project. For this project I researched IPv6. IPv6 seemed super interesting to me because it allows uncomprehendable amounts of people on the internet, and allows them to communicate with each other. The Internet Protocol (IP) is a standard protocol used across every device connected to the internet. It allows for communication between different devices, and is not dependent on the users location since it is through the internet. It allows communication within seconds to another person or group of people. IPv6 was created to combat the rapid increase of devices connected to the internet. It allowed a lot more devices to be connected at once. This will help through the rapid population growth as it will allow more people to be connected and online.

IP is the address of your device to which is connected to the internet. The previous version of IP addresses 4,294,967,296 devices connected to the internet. The reason for the upgrade is the amount of new devices being connected".
